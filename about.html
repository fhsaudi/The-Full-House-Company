OUR BEGINNING

Full House was started in 2014 by the founders of SAAM Group, which was established in 1985, to cater to the building materials industry's growing needs. Our story began as a small establishment with a big dream. The founders, driven by their passion for construction and understanding of the market, saw an opportunity to make a difference. They envisioned a company that provided high-quality building materials while prioritizing customer satisfaction.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown steadily, always maintaining our commitment to excellence and innovation. Our journey has been one of dedication and hard work.

OUR VISION

Our vision at Full House is to be the leading provider of building materials, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide top-quality building materials that enhance the success of every project we support. We are dedicated to understanding and meeting the unique needs of our customers, ensuring they receive the best products and services in the industry.

Our journey has been one of passion and dedication. From our humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a trusted name in the building materials industry.
