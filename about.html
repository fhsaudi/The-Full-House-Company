OUR BEGINNING                   

Full House was started in 2014 by the founders of SAAM Group, established in 1985, to cater to the growing needs of the building materials industry. Our story began as a small establishment with a big dream. The founders, driven by their passion for construction and a deep understanding of the market, saw an opportunity to make a difference in the industry. They envisioned a company that not only provided high-quality building materials but also prioritized the needs and satisfaction of its customers.

From our humble beginnings, we have grown steadily, always maintaining our commitment to excellence and innovation. Our journey has been one of dedication, hard work, and a relentless pursuit of quality. Over the years, we have expanded our product range and services, continually adapting to the changing needs of the construction sector. Our growth is a testament to the trust and loyalty of our customers, who have been with us every step of the way.

OUR VISION

Our vision at Full House is to be the leading provider of building materials, recognized for our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction. We aim to continuously evolve and expand, setting new standards in the industry while maintaining our core values of reliability, affordability, and exceptional service.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to provide top-quality building materials that enhance the success of every project we support. We are dedicated to understanding and meeting the unique needs of our customers, ensuring they receive the best products and services in the industry. By prioritizing customer satisfaction, maintaining rigorous quality standards, and continuously improving our processes, we aim to build lasting relationships and contribute to the growth and success of our clients' construction endeavors.
