WHO WE ARE

The founders of Full House, part of the esteemed SAAM Group, recognized a pressing need for a quality building materials showroom in Abqaiq. Driven by a passion for excellence and a commitment to providing top-tier construction materials, they established Fullhouse. Our mission is to serve the local and regional construction industry with a wide array of superior products, including pavers, wall systems, landscape products, cement products, construction stones, natural stones, electrical, and plumbing materials.
